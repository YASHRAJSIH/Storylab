Economic recovery and policy are intricately connected, as the current state of the economy significantly influences the direction and implementation of energy and climate policies. In France, the expansion of renewable energy sources and the installation of heating systems have been key aspects of the energy transition strategy. However, the progress of these initiatives has varied between countries. For instance, France experienced faster growth in the installation of heating systems emitting more than 300 grams of CO2 equivalent per kilowatt hour compared to Germany. This difference can be attributed to the distinct policy approaches and economic conditions in each country.
