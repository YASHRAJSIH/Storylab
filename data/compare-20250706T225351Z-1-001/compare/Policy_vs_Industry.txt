Policy and industry are connected in the report through the discussion of the need for major restructuring in the construction industry, particularly in densifying urban areas, to relieve housing shortages. This is in line with the French government's decarbonization strategy (SNBC), which aims to reduce emissions in various sectors, including industry, transport, and buildings. The report notes that France's emissions budgets by sector indicate that industry is a significant contributor to emissions, and the current decarbonization strategy has lower emissions budgets for the period from 2024 to 2028 compared to the previous strategy. This suggests that policy makers should support restructuring in the industry to reduce emissions and meet decarbonization targets.
